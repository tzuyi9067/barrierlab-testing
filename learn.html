How to use each product

A quick field guide.

Each product has a simple routine. Put them together and they're the Barrier Lab system. Give the inside line 6–8 weeks to show up in the mirror — probiotics aren't rescue products.

◔
BL-01 · Outside

Recovery Balm

  1. Cleanse with a fragrance-free wash. Pat skin damp.
  2. Within 3 min, apply a pea-sized dab to each patch.
  3. Layer any prescription Rx first, then seal with balm.
  4. Use morning + night. Expect visible improvement in 5–10 days.
▤
BL-02 · Inside · Kids

Inner Powder

  1. One sachet per day, for ages 3+.
  2. Stir into 4–6 oz cool or room-temp liquid (water, milk, juice).
  3. Give with a meal. Consistency matters more than timing.
  4. Expect skin changes in 6–8 weeks of daily use.
◈
BL-03 · Inside · Adult

Inner Barrier Complex

  1. Three capsules per day (we used to recommend two; new research supports three).
  2. Take with food — the lactoferrin absorbs best with a meal.
  3. Space 2 hours from antibiotics if you're on any.
  4. Expect skin changes in 6–8 weeks of daily use.
