Powder Hero

L-Histidine

Precursor to filaggrin — the protein that holds the skin barrier together.

Dose · 500 mg per sachet

Fermentation-derived, vegan L-histidine — the form studied in atopic-dermatitis trials.

Algal DHA

A clean-source omega-3 — the barrier's anti-inflammatory fuel.

Dose · 100 mg per sachet

Schizochytrium-derived algal DHA — vegan, fish-free, heavy-metal-tested.

Vitamin D3

Supports immune regulation in the skin; levels often run low in eczema-prone kids.

Dose · 600 IU per sachet

Lichen-sourced cholecalciferol — the natural D3 form (not synthetic D2) and fully vegan.

Zinc

Micronutrient for immune function and skin cell turnover.

Dose · 5 mg per sachet

Zinc glycinate — chelated to the amino acid glycine for gentler absorption than zinc oxide.

Biotin

B-vitamin involved in the fatty acid synthesis the skin uses for barrier lipids.

Dose · 75 mcg per sachet

D-biotin — the naturally active isomer, at a pediatric dose (not the megadoses in beauty gummies).

L-Glutamine

The gut lining's favorite amino acid — supports the skin-gut axis.

Dose · 250 mg per sachet

Fermentation-derived, vegan L-glutamine — the free-form version absorbed straight from the gut.

Prebiotic GOS

Galacto-oligosaccharides — food for the friendly gut bacteria linked to eczema severity.

Dose · 2 g per sachet

Short-chain GOS — the same prebiotic fiber found in human breast milk.
