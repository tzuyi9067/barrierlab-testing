• BL-01 · The Recovery Balm

Barrier-repairing lipids that work with your skin.

The Recovery Balm is formulated around the 3:1:1 lipid ratio — the same ceramide-cholesterol-fatty-acid mix your stratum corneum already uses — so the formula rebuilds the wall instead of sitting on top of it.

Balm Exclusive

Ceramide NP

The lipid your eczema-prone skin is missing. Rebuilds the intercellular "mortar" between skin cells.

Why it's essential

Matches the phytosphingosine backbone of your skin's own ceramides — a plant-derived, identical-to-skin form.

Cholesterol

A structural lipid that keeps ceramides organized in the right ratio.

Bioavailable form

Ultra-pure, non-animal cholesterol — delivered in the clinical 3:1:1 ratio with ceramides and fatty acids.

Fatty Acids

The third member of the barrier lipid triad — seals the gaps between bricks.

Bioavailable form

Plant-derived free fatty acids (linoleic, palmitic, stearic) — the exact chain lengths found in healthy skin.

Colloidal Oatmeal

FDA-recognized skin protectant that calms itch on contact.

Why it's essential

Finely-milled whole oat, delivering avenanthramides — the anti-itch molecules unique to oats.

Madecassoside

A molecule from Centella asiatica that quiets visible redness.

Bioavailable form

Purified triterpenoid from Centella — clinically studied at 0.1–1% for atopic-prone skin.

Allantoin

Softens flakes and helps dull rough patches let go.

Why it's essential

A gentle keratolytic — helps the skin shed stuck, rough cells without the sting of acids.

Glycerin

The humectant skin researchers keep coming back to. Draws water into the stratum corneum.

Bioavailable form

Vegetable-derived, 99.7% pure glycerin at an effective 5%+ dose.

Panthenol (Pro-Vit B5)

Penetrates into the epidermis, converts to Vitamin B5, soothes inflamed skin.

Bioavailable form

D-panthenol — the alcohol form that skin converts into active pantothenic acid (Vit B5).

Hyaluronic Acid

Binds up to 1,000× its weight in water inside the skin.

Bioavailable form

A blend of low- and high-molecular-weight HA — hydrates at the surface and deeper in the epidermis.

Squalane

Plant-derived occlusive that mimics the oil your skin already makes.

Bioavailable form

100% plant-based squalane from sugarcane — identical to the squalene in human sebum, but shelf-stable.

Shea Butter

A traditional occlusive rich in Vitamin E and barrier-supporting fatty acids.

Why it's essential

Unrefined, fair-trade West African shea — delivers oleic and stearic acids plus natural tocopherols.

How to use

Three steps. Twice a day.

01

Cleanse gently

Fragrance-free wash, lukewarm water. Pat — don't rub — the skin mostly dry.

02

Apply while damp

A pea-sized dab on each patch within 3 minutes of drying off. Warm between fingers, press into skin.

03

Layer any Rx

If using a prescription, apply Rx first, then seal with Recovery Balm. Morning + night, every day.
